With over 7 million residents living in cities like San Francisco, Oakland, and San Jose, the Bay Area is a vast and geographically diverse area that surrounds the San Francisco Bay in northern California.The Bay Area comprises parts of nine counties: Marin, Sonoma, Napa, Solano, San Francisco, San Mateo, Contra Costa, Alameda, and Santa Clara, though it lacks formal boundaries. The Peninsula of the Bay Area is made up of the counties of San Mateo and the northern portion of Santa Clara, while the East Bay is made up of the counties of Contra Costa and Alameda. Sonoma and Napa counties' southern regions are included in the Bay Area.The Bay Area, a relatively small area in itself, still has distinct areas with their own cultures and attractions.
